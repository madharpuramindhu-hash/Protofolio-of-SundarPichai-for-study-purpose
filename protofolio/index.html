About

The man behind Google

From Chennai to Silicon Valley

Sundar Pichai was born in 1972 in Chennai, India. Growing up, his family didn't own a car or a TV.

He studied at IIT Kharagpur, earned a scholarship to Stanford, then got his MBA from Wharton.

He joined Google in 2004, launched Chrome in 2008, and became CEO in 2015.
